(constant) buildBlock

Constructs a new building block or dome on the targeted space. Ends the current player's turn and passes the turn to the opponent. Also checks if the opponent is trapped with zero legal moves, triggering a blockade win condition.

(constant) getValidBuilds

Calculates all adjacent spaces where a worker can construct a building block or dome. Enforces the rule that buildings cannot be constructed on spaces occupied by workers or on top of completed domes.
Source:
  • santorini.js, line 170

(constant) getValidMoves

Identifies all valid adjacent spaces a worker can move into. Enforces Santorini movement rules: workers can step up a maximum of one level, but can drop down any number of levels. Workers cannot move into spaces occupied by other workers or completed domes.
Source:
  • santorini.js, line 144

(constant) moveWorker

Relocates the active worker to a new space on the board. If the worker steps up onto a Level 3 building, the current player immediately wins. Otherwise, the turn transitions to the building phase.
Source:
  • santorini.js, line 215

(constant) placeWorker

Places a new worker onto the board during the initial setup phase. Players alternate placing workers. Once all four workers are placed, the game transitions into Player 1's first official turn.
Source:
  • santorini.js, line 318

(constant) selectWorker

Marks a specific worker as the active worker for the current turn, transitioning the game phase from worker selection to movement.
